HOW IT WORKS

AlyBoom was created to enhance children’s lives with disabilities through music.

Donate

Because Music is Universal Language

able to be interpreted regardless of language or cognitive barriers, it creates a unique connection. By giving these children a powerful tool that can build their communications, motor and social skills, music provides an effective way for them to understand themselves, other people, and the word around them.

Regardless of the special need. Music has the ability to totally transform lives for the better, because it effortlessly generates a safe learning environment and according to Barbara Crowe, former President of the National Association for Music Therapy ( which is now called the American Music Therapy Association), it can bridge the gap ‘between withdrawal and awareness… isolation and interaction’.

Using music for special needs children offers a positive way to motivate behavior and improve on all skill levels.

Every donation to our program/raffle provides the funds to create our music programs, performances and field trips.
