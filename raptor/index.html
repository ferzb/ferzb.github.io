About us

We’re the Bengaluru Raptors, one of the top teams in India’s Premier Badminton League (PBL). The team is owned by Matrix Badminton Teamworks Pvt. Ltd. Runner’s up in the last year’s edition, we are back with a bang this year with a top-notch team. Getting in some of the biggest badminton talent into the Raptor pack, we are looking forward to making a dent in this year’s league. Do join us and cheer us on! #goraptors
